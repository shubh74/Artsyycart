ABOUT THE ARTIST

Sneha Gajera

Sneha Gajera is a passionate, inspiring, and highly skilled artist and educator. She has dedicated her life to nurturing creativity in others, teaching hundreds of students across all ages and backgrounds. Her classes are known for their warmth, encouragement, and hands-on approach, making art accessible and joyful for everyone.

  • 2+ years of experience teaching art
  • Specializes in Pop Art, Resin Art, Quilling, and Sketching
  • Conducts engaging workshops and personalized classes
  • Encourages creativity, confidence, and self-expression
  • Her students describe her as motivating, patient, and truly inspiring
“Sneha doesn’t just teach art—she helps you to discover your own creative voice. Her passion and positivity make every class a joy!”
